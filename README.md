# Revenue Metric Analysis (2022)
Data analytics project focused on revenue metrics including MRR, churn rate, ARPPU, and customer segmentation.

## Project Overview
- The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue trends, churn, and customer segmentation.
- The dashboard enables quick analysis of revenue performance and customer lifecycle dynamics.

## Tools Used
- SQL (Data Analysis & Aggregations)
- Tableau (Data Visualization & Dashboard)
  
## Key Analysis Performed
- Analyzed monthly revenue trends to identify growth patterns
- Broke down revenue into MRR components (new, expansion, churn, contraction)
- Calculated and evaluated churn rate and its revenue impact
- Segmented users by lifecycle stages (new, active, churn, inactive, back)
- Assessed ARPPU to understand user value
- Compared expansion vs contraction to measure revenue health
- Analyzed age groups for revenue contribution

## Key Insights
- Churn Rate is relatively high (~34%), indicating a significant loss of customers that directly impacts revenue stability.
- Expansion MRR partially offsets churn, but not consistently enough to drive strong net growth.
- Customer growth peaks mid-year, followed by a slowdown, suggesting potential seasonality or acquisition challenges.
- ARPPU remains relatively stable, meaning revenue changes are mostly driven by user count rather than spending behavior.
- Back-from-churn users increase toward the end of the year, highlighting opportunities for re-engagement strategies.
- Younger age groups (14–24) contribute the largest share of revenue, indicating a key target segment.
